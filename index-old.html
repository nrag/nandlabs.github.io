I have worked in Microsoft for 10 years as an Engineering Manager, Architect, Technical Lead, and Individual Contributor in various teams.

  • Windows Update Built Peer-to-Peer systems for Windows Update downloads
  • Office 365 Email Worked in various capacities in Office 365 service for over 5 years in Email search, Collaboration tools and storage.
  • Office 365 Search Played an important role as an Individual Contributor in replacing the indexing engine in Exchange 2013.
  • Office 365 eDiscovery Technical Lead for the In-Place eDiscovery that built solutions to search PB of data distributed across thousands of users in interactive time. At the core of these solutions is the distributed search infrastructure to search thousands of users distributed across thousands of backend servers.
  • Office 365 Collaboration Managed the team that built and scaled Public Folders for the Office 365 service. Public Folders is a collaboration tool in Exchange that has existed since the 1990s. My team re-engineered public folders for the Office 365 service and scaled it.
  • Office 365 Collaboration As the Engineering Manager started Unified Groups, the new collaboration experience for the Office 365 service. The team was responsible for building and scaling the backend services responsible for identity and access, federated resource provisioning, comet notifications and search.
  • Office 365 OneDrive As an architect for the OneDrive search team, I'm responsible for building a search experience for both OneDrive for Consumer and OneDrive for Business.
